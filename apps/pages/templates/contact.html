Our Story

The 210-acre Diakonia Center is the only Orthodox Christian retreat center in the Southeast and one of only a few in the United States. In 2003, His Eminence Metropolitan Alexios, working with his Metropolitan Council, established the Diakonia Center to help achieve important goals:

• To provide an environment where the faithful can commune with nature
• To provide a retreat center for all age groups and interests
• To create a village of buildings tailored to meet the needs of all visitors

Since its establishment in 2003, more than a thousand people a year have visited the Diakonia Center, helping the Metropolis achieve each of its goals.

THINGS TO DO

There are tons of things to do here on our campus. There’s hiking, fishing, canoeing, swimming, basketball, volleyball, soccer and much more. There’s also many wonderful waterfalls and hiking in the area. Learn more.
